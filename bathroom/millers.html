Miller's Crossing - 7/10

Miller's always has a nice bathroom to use. Not great, but not bad. If I were unwell, I'd probably pick this bathroom to use. Its always cold, and it has appropriate sizing. There are two stalls, a waiting area, and a sink area. The mirror is really cute (bonus points). Their bathroom tends to be on the messier side, however. It is also so close to the actual bar that I'm pretty sure you could smell it at some point. The bathroom is rough to wait for when it gets busy, but it's nothing like The Western. I also don't feel as gross leaving the bathroom in Miller's. Overall, it's a pretty neat bathroom.


Due to how close it is to the bar but how cute the mirror is, Miller's gets a 7/10. Of all the options, Miller's would be my runner-up after The Hawthorn. 7/10.
