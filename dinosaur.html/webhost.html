Web Host Research Assignment

  • Bluehost: Bluehost has many plans such as 'Basic', 'Choice Plus', 'Online Store', and 'Pro'. These plans vary from $2.95-$13.95 per month. They also have three web hosting options. These include 'Dedicated Hosting', 'Shared Hosting', and 'VPS Hosting'. Additionally, they do have free domain options, but it also depends on the plan choice.
  • HostGator: HostGator only has three plans: 'Heathcling', 'Baby', and 'eCommerce'. These plans are currently having deals ranging from 68% off to 76% off; however, they normally range from $2.52 to $4.67. They have four hosting plans for Shared hosting, WordPress hosting, VPS hosting, and Dedicated hosting. They also have free domains for one year once you sign up for a plan.
  • GoDaddy: GoDaddy is the most expensive option with prices ranging from $2.99 to $14.99. Their web hosting plans are 'Economy', 'Deluxe', and 'Ultimate'. At their most basic plan, Economy, they offer a free domain. You also have the option to build a website among other services.
